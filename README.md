# Obsidian Templates
Templates for [Obsidian][1] notes. I have two distinct vaults: A personal vault on my personal devices, and a work vault on my work devices. My work role is a mix of trainer, mentor, and coach for a UK apprenticeship and this is reflected in the type of information recorded.

## Inspiration
The metadata for the notes is structured to be used alongside the [Bases][2] Obsidian plugin. This allows for views of notes for indiviual apprentices, whole cohorts, or even individual employers. This has been primarily infulenced by the vault-style of Obsidian developer [Steph Ango][3].

In his post [How I use Obsidian][4] he outlines his approach to vault management and how he uses Bases. Steph's overarching philosophy seems to be one of reducing the friction of using Obsidian as much as possible. The main way he does this is by keeping the majority of his notes in the root folder and organising them using the rich metadata he adds to each one. Even this is simplified through the extensive use of a large range of templates, resulting in a lot less time populating the note metadata. I recommend you read it to understand more of why I have structued things the way I have.

This is my collection of templates.
